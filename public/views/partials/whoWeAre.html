Who We Are

We live in a society that expects reliable wireless communications and data transfer everywhere we go. From beautiful downtowns with buildings several hundred feet tall to farmlands in the Midwest plains, wireless infrastructure is at the forefront of humanity’s expansion plans not only across North America, but the entire world.

Here at One Way Building Services in Edina, MN we’re proud of our 40-year history serving countless customers across the Midwest and beyond. We are a premier provider of telecommunications infrastructure services and continue to be on the cutting edge of new wireless technologies and implementations across the United States. Although we specialize in telecommunications, we work on anything from cell phone towers to general construction projects. You name it, we can build it.

With several departments under one roof, One Way is uniquely capable of serving as an all-encompassing provider of telecommunications construction and maintenance including, but not limited to civil engineering, electrical engineering, welding, and crane services. All of our departments collaboratively work together to ensure a smooth and successful process from the initial planning phase through project completion. We not only believe in true teamwork, we live it every day!

When working with One Way as a client, we consider you an essential member of our team. We treat our business partners with the utmost respect and encourage strong communication between all parties. One Way believes in long-term, mutually beneficial business partnerships, continuing to value personal connections and the benefits of in-person meetings and lively discussions. This is what we are known for, and this passion drives not only ours, but our client’s success.

We believe in the old adage under promise – over deliver. Our work speaks for itself and our reputation in the Midwest is one we are quite proud of. Our management style is simple but effective, all work or effort is to support those out in the field, the invaluable employees who work at varying heights in all weather conditions.

That’s our One Way. The Right Way.

Building Our Company From The Ground Up

Gary Bormes

President & CEO

A proud Navy Veteran, Gary started One Way Building Services, Inc. 40 years ago in 1977 on his strong business sense and dedication to doing things “One Way – the Right Way”. Doing business “The Right Way” is not just a motto, but a major part of One Way’s culture and is greatly credited with the Company’s longevity and substantial growth over the past 40 years.

In 1996 during a conversation with a friend on a softball diamond, Gary was asked about his experience with building telecommunications towers. Never having built a telecommunications tower, Gary decided to learn more about this relatively new technology, eventually driving One Way Building Services down a highly specialized path of building and maintaining telecommunications infrastructure. Now, over 20 years since that conversation on the ball field, One Way Building Services is the business partner of choice to all major wireless carriers in the Midwest and beyond.
