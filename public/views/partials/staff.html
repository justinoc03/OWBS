Gary Bormes

President & CEO

A proud Navy Veteran, Gary started One Way Building Services, Inc. 40 years ago in 1977 on his strong business sense and dedication to doing things “One Way – the Right Way”. Doing business “The Right Way” is not just a motto, but is a major part of One Way’s culture and is greatly credited with the Company’s longevity and substantial growth over the past 40 years.

In 1996 during a conversation with a friend on a softball diamond, Gary was asked about building telecommunications towers. Never having built a telecommunications tower did not stop Gary, it only encouraged him to dig deeper to learn more about this new opportunity. Now, over 20 years since that conversation on the ball field, One Way Building Services is the business partner of choice to all major carriers in the Midwest and beyond.

Gary’s business approach has always been to hire the right people and allow them the autonomy to grow and excel within One Way, resulting in a truly collaborative and rewarding business culture for all employees and business partners alike.
