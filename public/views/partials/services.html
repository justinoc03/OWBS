Distributed Antenna System (DAS)

When people think of wireless signals, they think of towers scattered across a landscape. But when it comes to concentrated crowds, DAS gets it right by utilizing a network of smaller cells that work together to create a strong, seamless wireless network. Used in stadiums and large buildings, DAS allows users to never lose connection and maintain fast data transfer rates meaning no dead spots, and no missed important emails or calls even when there are thousands of devices on the network. DAS does all of this, plus there is no need for an unsightly cell tower.

Call One Way Building Services now to talk with our professionals about our DAS services, we get DAS done The Right Way.
